TOWN / BOSS NPC

List below are NPCs exclusively in Calamity Mod, scaling on Vanilla NPCs also changes depending on difficulties

- NPCs -

Not to be confused with Terraria NPCs.

The Calamity Mod adds five friendly NPCs to provide the player with a multitude of weapons and buffs in progression. These NPCs only move to the player's house once their requirements are met.

Pre-Hardmode

NPC Requirement Infomation
Sea King Giant Clam has been defeated Sells a set of water-themed weapons and gear, provides some lore-based information , and cast Amidas's blessing bubble (buff) for player.
Bandit Player has beaten da bonez-tron and got al least 1 Platinum coin in their wallet Sells a bunch of rogue weapons and equipment related, refunds player after using Goblin Tinkerer's reforge

Hardmode

NPC Requirement Infomation
Archmage Cryogen has been defeated Sells a set of ice-themed weapons and gear.
Drunk Princess Fabsol's Vodka exists in player's inventory An alcoholic maniac, sells several "fermented" potions and candles which grant unique buffs and displays the player's deaths.

Post-Memelord

NPC Requirement Infomation
Brimstone
Witch		 Supreme Calamitas has been defeated Allows the player to enchant their weapons at a high cost, giving the weapons new abilities but with drawbacks.
