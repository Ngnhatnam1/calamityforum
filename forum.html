Bosses are unbalanced right now (Rev + Master)

The bosses that weren't heavily changed in this update are extremely easy compared to the ones that were, and skipping bosses has never been more incentivized.

Examples I can think of off the top of my head: EoW and especially BoC are way harder than HM/Perferators in their current state (Brain more so than EoW). WoF and Slime God are cake walks compared to Skeletron. I wouldn't blame people for skipping him. Calamitas Clone is way way easier than mechs, especially Prime. Can easily do it before them. Ravager is a joke compared to Golem now and I see no reason not to fight him first to give yourself an edge.

I know that they're trying to up the difficulty for master mode and I don't have a problem with that. My problem is how right now the difficulty will suddenly spike out of nowhere and bosses later in progression will be much easier compared to what you are currently on. Say what you will about boss progression before this update but at least it felt more linear and coherent. If they want to commit to buffing up bosses for master, then they should touch up all of them, not just a select few.
